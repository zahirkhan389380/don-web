LADAKH FOOD DONATION,

Our mission is to bridge the gap between abundance and need by redistributing surplus food from hotels
to those who need it most in Ladakh. Many hotels often have leftover food that is still perfectly good to eat. Instead of letting
this food go to waste, we collect and distribute it to local communities, ensuring that nutritious meals reach those
who are struggling with food insecurity. Through this initiative, we aim to support the health and well-being of our fellow residents while also promoting sustainability
and reducing waste. Join us in making a difference, one meal at a time.
